Joe Doe's Adventure

Joe decides to explore Germany!

All those dusty self help books on his shelf had an effect after all, Joe pats himself on the back for living in the moment!

Joe has never even been outside of his hometown, let alone in another country. So he's not sure where to start, but a taxi stops in front of him and he jumps in.

Joe takes a taxi to the nearest town and gets out in the town square. What should Joe do for fun?

There's a pretzel stand on the corner, and a tour bus about to leave across the street.
