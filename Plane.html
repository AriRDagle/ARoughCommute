Joe Doe's Adventure

Joe got on a plane

OH NO!

The plane is taking Joe to Germany!

Joe got distracted in the airport and picked up someone elses ticket! The plane is already in the air when he hears his neighbors talking about OktoberFest.

Joe has a history of not handling stress very well. Is he capable of change?

Will he embrace his mistake and call out of work to explore Germany? Or will he PANIC!
