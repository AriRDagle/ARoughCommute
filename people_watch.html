Joe Doe's Adventure

Joe takes a deep breath, and tries to calm himself down.

One of Joe's favorite ways to relax is to people watch! He likes to look at a stranger and make up stories about their lives.

There's a homeless man singing to himself in the corner, and a mother and child chattering to each other sitting across from him.

Will he calm himself by thinking about the homeless man? or the family?
