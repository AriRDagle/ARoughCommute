Joe Doe's Adventure

Joe realizes with a start that he's on the wrong plane! Joe doesn't handle stress very well.

Joe begins to have a panic attack, he's breathing harder and harder until he starts hyperventilating. The flight attendant is making his way along the aisle with the drink cart, when he reaches Joe, the cart crashes into the back of his seat with a BANG.

How will Joe react?

Will he break down and scream? Or yell and YELL and Y E L L
